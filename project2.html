Model Instructions

  • w, a, s, d: Rotate the camera on its axis
  • Up Arrow + Down Arrow: Move the camera forward, and backward
  • Dog 1 Jump: Makes the Dog jump.
  • Dog Run Faster!!: Increases the speed of the dog.
  • Stop Running!: Stops the dog's movement.
  • Make Orthographic: Switches to orthographic view.
  • Make Perspective: Switches to perspective view.
  • Slider Eye Z: Adjusts the eye position along the Z-axis.
  • Slider Center Z: Adjusts the center position along the Z-axis.
  • Additional Information: The cat plays with the ball independently.
  • Additional Information: The UFOs move independently.
