Model Instructions

  • w, a, s, d: Rotate the camera on its axis
  • Up Arrow + Down Arror: Move the camera in forward, and backwards in its direction
  • Dog 1 Jump: Makes the Dog jump.
  • Dog Run Faster!!: Increases the speed of the dog if it is running, or makes the dog start running.
  • Stop Running!: Stops the dog's movement.
  • Make Orthographic: Switches to orthographic view.
  • Make Perspective: Switches to perspective view.
  • Slider Eye Z: Adjusts the eye position along the Z-axis.
  • Slider Center Z: Adjusts the center position along the Z-axis.
  • Additional Information: The cat plays with the ball, independently with no instruction
  • Additional Information: The UFO (cube) with a rotating handle also moves independently, as well as the rectangular UFO
